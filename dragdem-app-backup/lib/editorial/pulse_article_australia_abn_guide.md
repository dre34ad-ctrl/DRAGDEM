## 1. The ABN: Your Professional ID Card

An **ABN (Australian Business Number)** is a unique 11-digit identifier issued by the Australian Taxation Office (ATO). For drag performers, operating as a "Sole Trader" with an active ABN is the standard for professional legitimacy.

**The "No-ABN" Trap:**
In Australia, if you do not provide a valid ABN to a venue or seeker, they are legally required to withhold **47%** of your payment to cover potential tax liabilities. This can severely impact your cash flow and brand reputation.

**The DRAGDEM Solution:**
Verify your ABN directly on your **DRAGDEM Dashboard**. Once verified, our system:
*   **Ensures Full Payouts:** Automatically signals to seekers that no withholding is required.
*   **Builds Seeker Trust:** Hospitality groups like **Merivale** and **The Imperial** prioritize ABN-verified talent to minimize their own administrative overhead.

## 2. GST: Managing the Threshold

If your annual turnover in Australia reaches **$75,000 AUD**, you are legally required to register for **Goods and Services Tax (GST)**.

*   **Pricing Clarity:** DRAGDEM allows you to toggle your GST status. If you are registered, the system will automatically include the 10% GST on your invoices, ensuring your accounting is flawless and your seekers know exactly what they are paying for.

## 3. Superannuation: Protecting Your Future

A major shift in Australian law now requires that many contractors be paid **Superannuation** (currently 11.5%) if they are performing "labor-only" contracts. This has become a significant concern for venues.

**How DRAGDEM Mitigates Risk:**
To protect both performers and seekers, DRAGDEM uses **Result-Based Contract Templates**. By framing bookings as the delivery of a specific "Act" or "Show" rather than a block of hourly labor, we help clarify your status as an independent professional, ensuring you and your clients are compliant with ATO guidelines.
